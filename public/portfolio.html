01 Lifestyle & Hospitality

La Maison Dorée — Brand Identity & Social Launch

The Challenge

A boutique Parisian-inspired hotel in Tel Aviv had no social presence and a visual language that failed to reflect its intimate luxury positioning. The brand needed a full social media management strategy that could compete with established lifestyle properties while feeling distinctly personal and artisan.

Creative Direction

Aviv developed an editorial content system built around golden-hour photography, intimate table settings, and a muted warm palette echoing the property's interiors. Every caption was crafted to feel like a handwritten note — inviting, specific, and sensory. Story formats introduced behind-the-scenes moments to humanise the brand.

Content Outcome

Within 90 days: 340% increase in profile visits, 4.8× growth in saves and shares, and a measurable uptick in direct booking enquiries attributed to Instagram. The feed became a destination in itself — consistently cited by guests as the reason they reached out.

340% Profile Visit Growth
4.8× Saves & Shares
90 Days to Results
02 Culinary E-Commerce

Terroir Goods — Content Strategy & E-Commerce Growth

The Challenge

A premium artisan food e-commerce brand had a world-class product but a fragmented visual identity across channels. Sales were driven almost entirely by word-of-mouth, with no cohesive social media management service in place to convert discovery into purchase. The brand needed content that educated, inspired, and sold — simultaneously.

Creative Direction

Aviv built a content ecosystem anchored in provenance storytelling — the origin of ingredients, the makers behind the products, and the rituals of use. Photography leaned into tactile textures: rough ceramics, golden oils, linen surfaces. Short-form video content was scripted and edited in-house to guide viewers from discovery to the product page in under 15 seconds.

Content Outcome

A 62% increase in website traffic from social channels within the first quarter, with a 28% conversion rate improvement on product pages linked from Instagram. Reels content regularly achieved 3–5× organic reach beyond the existing follower base, establishing the brand as a trusted voice in the gourmet lifestyle category.

62% Social Traffic Lift
28% Conversion Uplift
5× Organic Reach
